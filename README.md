# OrnnForge
OrnnForge: The Content Creator's Crucible

OrnnForge is an open-source platform that acts as a digital crucible for content creation. It allows creators to throw their raw ideas, media fragments, and inspiration into the forge, where they can be heated, hammered, and combined into something entirely new and powerful.

Just as the mythical demigod Ornn crafts legendary artifacts from base materials, this platform helps you forge your scattered thoughts and assets into polished, impactful content.

## Roadmap

### Phase 1: Community Discussion to Text/Image (Highest Priority)
- Crawl popular discussions from platforms like V2EX, Reddit, and Hupu
- Use AI technologies for content summarization, rewriting, and structuring
- Output to text/image platforms like Zhihu, Xiaohongshu, and Medium

### Phase 2: Video Content to Text/Image (Secondary Priority)
- Obtain video content from platforms like Bilibili, YouTube, Douyin, and TikTok
- Utilize speech recognition, keyframe extraction, and content summarization technologies
- Convert to in-depth text/image content suitable for platforms like Zhihu and Medium

### Phase 3: Community Discussion to Short Video (Medium Priority)
- Convert text discussions into short videos for platforms like Douyin and TikTok
- Require support from text-to-speech and video generation technologies

### Phase 4: Long Video to Short Video (Lower Priority)
- Automatically identify highlight clips or key points in long videos
- Generate essential content for short video platforms

## Technical Architecture Vision

- Multi-platform content collector (supporting major domestic and international content platforms)
- AI content processing engine (text summarization, speech recognition, video analysis)
- Content format converter (adapting to different platform content format requirements)
- Collaborative editing system (supporting multi-person collaborative creation)
- Workflow engine (supporting reusable content processing workflows)
